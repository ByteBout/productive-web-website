Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how ProductiveWeb, developed by ByteBout, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Our Commitment

ProductiveWeb is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe that tools meant to improve your digital wellbeing should respect your privacy completely.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data

Specifically:

  • No browsing history is collected
  • No personal information is gathered
  • No analytics or tracking tools are implemented
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No user accounts or authentication are required

How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely locally on your device by:

  • Injecting CSS styles to hide distracting elements on web pages
  • Using content scripts to modify the page structure (DOM manipulation)
  • Hiding elements like feeds, comments, shorts, and recommendations based on your preferences

All processing happens on your device. Nothing leaves your computer.

Local Storage

The extension stores your settings and preferences using your browser's sync storage. This means:

  • Your preferences are stored in your browser's sync storage
  • If you have browser sync enabled (e.g., Chrome Sync, Firefox Sync), your settings will sync across your devices
  • This syncing happens through your browser's own infrastructure (Google, Mozilla, etc.)
  • We never have access to this data - it's handled entirely by your browser
  • You can disable sync in your browser settings if you prefer to keep settings local to one device

All data remains within your browser's ecosystem. ByteBout does not operate any servers or receive any of this information.

Open Source

This extension is open-source. You can review the complete source code at Github to verify our privacy claims.
